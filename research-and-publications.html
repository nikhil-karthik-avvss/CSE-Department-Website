Research and Publications

The faculty members and students of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering are actively engaged in cutting-edge research across diverse areas of CSE. Research is carried out through internally and externally funded projects, collaborative works, and industry partnerships. The department has secured several patents and has published research in reputed journals and conferences including Elsevier, Springer, IEEE, and ACM.

With a strong focus on innovation, the department fosters interdisciplinary research, encourages student involvement in projects, and contributes to advancements in both theoretical foundations and real-world applications.
