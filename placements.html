Placement Statistics

The department is keen about inculcating employability skills for the students to succeed in landing dream job offers. A Mock placement drive for the III year students is organized every year to give an experience of real placement drives. For the past several years, our students have been placed in top companies like MotorQ, Adobe, Paypal, Amazon, Media.net, eBay, Equinix, Google, Avalara.
