Welcome to CSE Department

Empowering students with knowledge, innovation, and research excellence since 1996.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) was established in the year 1996 since the inception of the college. The primary objective of CSE department is to provide a high quality computer science education to students from all over India as well as abroad and across all economic strata. The department offers an integrated dual degree program M.Tech (CSE), an undergraduate degree program B.E(CSE) and a postgraduate degree program M.E(CSE). In 2006, the department was recognized as a Centre for Research, by Anna University to offer a Ph.D. program. The undergraduate program B.E (CSE) has been reaccredited by NBA in 2021 and the postgraduate program M.E (CSE) has been accredited by NBA in 2016. The department has state-of-the-art laboratory facilities, Wi-Fi enabled class rooms with laptops to facilitate e-Learning, a well-equipped seminar hall and a well-stocked department library.

VISION

To emerge as a world class technology department through education, innovation and collaborative research.

MISSION

  • To impart quality education to students.
  • To create and disseminate knowledge for the betterment of mankind.
  • To establish a center of excellence in collaboration with industries, research laboratories and other agencies to meet the changing needs of society.
  • To provide individual attention and enable character building.
  • To encourage entrepreneurship skills among students.

Department Highlights

  • Dedicated and highly qualified faculty members who inculcate both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in a balanced manner.

  • External funded projects completed with DST, AICTE, ISRO, Nvidia, and others.
